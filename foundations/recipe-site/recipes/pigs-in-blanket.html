Cheesy Pigs in a Blanket

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 pound bacon, each slice cut in half crosswise
  • 2 cans crescent roll dough
  • 8 ounces Cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 32 cornichon gherkins or other small pickles
  • 1 large egg, beaten

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with parchment.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium heat and cook bacon until slightly crispy but still pliable, about 5 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  3. Unroll crescent roll dough and split into triangles. Cut each triangle in half lengthwise to form 32 narrower triangles.
  4. Place about 1 tablespoon cheese on bottom half of each dough triangle. Top with a slice of bacon. Lay 1 pickle horizontally on the bottom of the triangle and roll up; place seam side down on the prepared baking sheet.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown and toasted, about 12 minutes.
